ABOUT Virtual Mannequin

Maxtron's Virtual Mannequins use cutting edge technology to project an holographic image and creates the illusion of a real life person, acting as an interactive informational and instructional tool for company owners. Virtual Mannequin provides high contrast, high resolution displays with ultra-wide viewing angles in all lighting conditions. A Virtual Mannequin is a display that will inform your customers and promote any offers your industry is offering. They are engaging and entertaining and most importantly they are new and exciting. They will absolutely stop customers in their tracks and make them notice!

HOW MAXTRON HELPS YOU?
