ABOUT STOCK INVENTORY SOFTWARE

Maxtron's Inventory management software is a computer-based system for tracking inventory levels, orders, sales and deliveries. It can also be used in the manufacturing industry to create a work order, bill of materials and other production-related documents. Companies use inventory management software to avoid product overstock and outages. It is a tool for organizing inventory data that before was generally stored in hardcopy form or in spreadsheets.

HOW MAXTRON HELPS YOU?

Our Inventory management software helps companies to reduce their carrying costs. The software is used to track products and parts as they are transported from a vendor to a warehouse, between warehouses, and finally to a retail location or directly to a customer. It also allows your employees to access up-to-date and reliable information on current inventory. Our software provides compatibility with multiple hardware's like barcode reader, printers, external devices etc.
